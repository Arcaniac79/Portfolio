About Me

I love learning new things, telling and writing stories and expressing my creativity in almost every way that I can find. I am fully equipped with the skills and knowledge to make something truly great. I am well versed with a wide variety of design software, ranging from web design, video editing, game development and curating social media content. As a designer, developer and storyteller my goal is to make the best experience possible for my audience and users.

My Projects

Path of Light

Path of Light is a Game Maker game that me and two friends made for the Pirate Game Jam 15 hosted by PirateSoftware. The theme was "Shadow and Alchemy" and this is what we came up with. All art assets, many sound assets and plenty of the code were all done by me.

LOTR Poster Concepts

This was a project I did for class where we made concepts of posters for our favourite movie trilogy. I chose Lord of the Rings, but I'd hope you could tell that without needing to be told. A rule we had was that we could only have 3 colours per poster, which I found was a very interesting restriction.

Self Cameo

This is another project I did in school where we had to take a picture of our selves and make a cameo of it. This was made with Adobe Illustrator and I tried to best capture my own features.
